About Our Founder

Qaisar Khan Tanoli

Senior Journalist | News Anchor | Sportsman

Qaisar Khan Tanoli is a Senior Journalist with nearly two decades of experience in journalism. Holding a Master’s degree in Mass Communication, he blends academic knowledge with extensive practical expertise.

He has worked with prestigious international platforms like CNN and CBS, which adds exceptional authenticity, credibility, and in-depth understanding to his reporting. His remarkable ability as an anchorperson has led him to conduct high-profile interviews with the Prime Minister and President of Pakistan.

Apart from journalism, Qaisar Khan Tanoli has excelled in sports, particularly in cricket, where he represented the U19 team and earned numerous awards for his outstanding performance.

Additionally, he possesses strong skills in networking and public relations. His reputation for being kind, approachable, and building meaningful connections has made him a respected figure both in media and social circles.

About Our Co-Founder

Hamza Khan Tanoli

Web Developer | IT Expert | Sportsman

Hamza Khan Tanoli is a young visionary who combines his passion for technology, sports, and humanitarian efforts. At just 21 years old, he has already made a significant impact in multiple fields.

Currently pursuing his studies at Bahria University Islamabad, Hamza is an accomplished web developer and IT expert with a strong background in software development. His dedication to learning is reflected in the multiple certifications he has earned in software development and programming.

Beyond the tech world, Hamza is a professional swimmer and cricketer, with a collection of trophies and achievements in competitive cricket. His love for sports extends to horse riding, a hobby he passionately pursues alongside his studies and career.

Despite his diverse talents, what truly sets Hamza apart is his kind and supportive nature. Driven by his love for humanity, he co-founded Ghulab Jan Welfare Trust as a selfless act of charity and well-being for all. His dedication to social welfare, combined with his expertise in IT and networking, ensures that help reaches those in need effectively and compassionately.
